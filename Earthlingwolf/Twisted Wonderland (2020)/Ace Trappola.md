# Ace Trappola

## Pe

*   Irresponsible - always scheming for ways to avoid work or responsibilities - _Ne_ escapism.
    
*   Lazy - often enters situations on a whim, only to give up or change his mind once sustained effort is demanded.
    
*   Interests: Basketball and magic tricks, especially card manipulation.
    
*   Jokes, banter, and humorous imitations.
    
*   Mimicry - able to quickly learn new skills simply by observing others - learned sleight of hand by watching his older brother and copying his movements.
    
*   Unique magic: Joker Snatch/Joker's Wild - allows him to steal another's unique magic.
    

## Ji

*   Independent - resistant to shaming and peer pressure, but easily swayed by appeal to his vanity.
    

## Je

*   His best subject is enigmics, which is basically the magical equivalent of physics - good casual reasoning.
